The national flag of France, known to English speakers as the French Tricolour, features three vertical bands coloured blue (hoist side), white, and red.

Liberté, égalité, fraternité, French for "liberty, equality, fraternity", is the national motto of France. 1

France's signage fonts include L1, L2, L4, and L5. 2
